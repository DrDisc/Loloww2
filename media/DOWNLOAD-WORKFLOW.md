### Step 4: NARA Catalog Search - Philippine Scouts (1-2 hours)

**This is your treasure trove - spend time here!**

1. Go to: https://catalog.archives.gov/
2. Click "Advanced Search"
3. **Search Parameters:**
   - Keywords: "Philippine Scouts"
   - Date Range: 1941-01-01 to 1942-12-31
   - Record Group: 111 (Signal Corps Photographs)
   - Digital Copies: Available Online Only (check this box!)
4. Click Search

**Alternative Searches (try each):**
- "26th Cavalry Regiment"
- "24th Field Artillery" AND "Philippines"
- "57th Infantry" AND "Philippine Scouts"
- "45th Infantry" AND "Philippine Scouts"
- "Bataan" AND "defenders" (1941-1942)
- "Philippine Division" AND "1941"

**For each result:**
1. Open the record
2. View digital copy
3. Download highest resolution available
4. **Record:** NARA ID number, title, date
5. Save to appropriate folder:
   - `/media/photos/philippine-scouts/` - General PS photos
   - `/media/photos/insignia/` - Close-ups of patches
   - `/media/photos/bataan/` - Bataan campaign
   - `/media/photos/medal-of-honor/` - MOH recipients

**Pro Tips:**
- Look for photos with "Signal Corps" photographer credits
- RG 111 is the mother lode for WWII photos
- Download TIFF if available (highest quality)
- Some photos have detailed captions - save those!

**Expected Downloads:** 15-30 photos (quality over quantity)

## üö´ Known Issues & Workarounds

### If PICRYL blocks you:
- Try incognito/private browsing
- Clear cookies
- Try different browser
- Download a few at a time, take breaks

### If NARA downloads fail:
- Some images require on-site visit to NARA
- Contact NARA Research Services: archives.gov/contact
- Try alternate sources (PICRYL often has NARA images)

### If Videvo requires payment:
- They have tiered licenses - free tier should work
- Alternative: Pexels also has some water buffalo footage
- Search "water buffalo" on other free sites

### If Philippine Scouts Heritage Society images are restricted:
- Email them requesting educational use permission
- Explain documentary nature of project
- Offer to credit them prominently
- Worst case: Use insignia as visual reference to commission recreation
