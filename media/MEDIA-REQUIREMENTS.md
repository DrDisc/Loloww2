## Legal & Ethical Considerations

### Copyright Status
- **Pre-1945 US Government photos:** Public domain
- **Military insignia (official):** Public domain if pre-1989
- **Private photographs:** May still be under copyright
- **Stock footage:** Requires licensing

### Usage Rights Needed
- **YouTube:** Need commercial or YouTube-safe license
- **TikTok/Instagram:** Check platform-specific licensing
- **Attribution requirements:** Track for all sources

### Ethical Guidelines
- **Emaciated soldiers photos:** Use respectfully, avoid exploitation
- **Death/suffering:** Contextual use only, not sensationalized
- **Cultural sensitivity:** Carabao imagery - honor Filipino cultural significance
- **Attribution:** Credit Philippine sources properly
