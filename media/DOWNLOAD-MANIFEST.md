## üö® IMPORTANT NOTES

### About PICRYL
- PICRYL is free and public domain
- No account required
- Sometimes slow to load - be patient
- If blocked, try incognito/private browsing

### About NARA Catalog
- Free access to public domain images
- No account needed for viewing/downloading
- High-resolution TIFFs often available
- Record NARA ID numbers for citation

### About Stock Footage
- **Videvo:** Free tier available, account required
- **Pexels:** No account needed, totally free
- Both allow commercial use
- No attribution required (but nice to credit)

### File Naming Convention
Follow this pattern:
- Photos: `[subject]_[detail]_[source]_[id].jpg`
- Footage: `[subject]_[duration]s_[source]_[number].mp4`
- Maps: `[location]_[type]_[source]_[year].jpg`

Examples:
- `scouts_uniform_carabao_patch_nara_111sc12345.jpg`
- `carabao_rice_field_15s_videvo_001.mp4`
- `bataan_defensive_lines_picryl_1942.jpg`
