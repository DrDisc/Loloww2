## üì∏ Historical Photographs - NARA

### NARA Philippine Archives Collection

**Main Collection Page:**
- **URL:** https://www.archives.gov/research/military/ww2/philippine
- **Size:** 1,665 boxes
- **Location:** National Archives, College Park, Maryland
- **Coverage:** Pacific War 1941-1942, Philippines operations

**Still Pictures Branch:**
- **Location:** National Archives, College Park, MD
- **Content:** 2+ million scans/digital images in NARA Catalog
- **Record Groups:**
  - **RG 111:** Signal Corps Photographs (ca. 1754 - ca. 1954)
  - **RG 407:** Records of the Adjutant General's Office
  - Index to Personalities in Signal Corps files (1940-1981)

**Search Portal:**
- **NARA Catalog:** https://catalog.archives.gov/
- **GetArchive (NARA):** https://nara.getarchive.net/

**Personnel Records:**
- Philippine Scouts service records: National Personnel Records Center, St. Louis, MO
