## Tactical Concepts Explained

**For audience unfamiliar with military tactics:**
- **Fields of Fire:** Overlapping areas where weapons can engage enemy
- **Pre-registered Artillery:** Artillery already aimed at key targets for quick response
- **Combined Arms:** Coordinating infantry, artillery, cavalry together
- **Counterattack:** Immediate attack against enemy penetration to restore defensive line

**Keep explanations simple** - use graphics to show concepts rather than heavy narration.
