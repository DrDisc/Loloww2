# Research Materials

This directory contains source materials, citations, and research notes for the Lolo's War project.

## Organization

- **philippine-sources/** - Materials from Philippine historians and archives
- **american-sources/** - US military records, archives, and historical accounts
- **japanese-sources/** - Japanese military records and historical perspectives
- **maps/** - Historical maps of campaigns and battle locations
- **timeline/** - Chronological research notes

## Research Status

Track research progress in `/sources/research-tracking.md`

## Guidelines

- Always cite sources with footnotes
- Cross-reference claims with multiple sources
- Note any conflicting accounts or interpretations
- Preserve original context when extracting quotes
