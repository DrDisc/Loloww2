### **/research/filipino-guerrilla-resistance-summary.md**

**Post-surrender resistance 1942-1944**

**What it covers**:
- Early resistance (Dec 1941 - April 1942) - even before surrender
- Major guerrilla organizations:
  - **The Huks** (Hukbalahap) - 15,000 fighters
  - **Marking Guerrillas** (Marcos Villa Agustín, boxer)
  - **Hunters ROTC Guerrillas** (students)
- Regional forces (Luzon, Mindanao, Visayas)
- Key figures:
  - **Ramon Magsaysay** (future President)
  - **Wendell Fertig** (American engineer, Mindanao commander)
  - **Macario Peralta Jr.** (Panay - almost liberated entire island)
  - **Vicente Lim** (tortured, executed for resistance)
- Role of released Filipino POWs (many joined guerrillas)
- Intelligence operations (crucial for 1944 liberation)
- Japanese response (brutal suppression)
- Impact and legacy

**Key point**: ~**260,000 Filipino guerrillas** active by liberation

**Use for**:
- Article section: "But resistance continued..."
- Context for your lolo's POW experience
- Showing Filipino heroism continued
- Connection to 1944 liberation
